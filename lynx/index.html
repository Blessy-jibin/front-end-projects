    Many are afraid of options - wrongly. Yes, with options you can very quickly lose a lot of money (or win) - but only if you absolutely want to take a high risk or does not know their functioning .

    Options are very diverse and if used properly and purposefully, neither dangerous nor speculative. Ultimately, it all depends on which goals you want to achieve: Options can be used for a variety of market situations and investment objectives .

    With options you can, for example, ...

    Protect your shares from a price decline

    Generate additional income for your existing portfolio

    benefit from falling or stagnant (sideways) markets

    benefit from rising markets without having to buy the appropriate number of shares

    benefit from a strong market movement , even if you do not know in which direction the prices will go

    Conclusion: Options can be used meaningfully in every market phase
